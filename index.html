Introduction

There are many different entries into the Fate Franchise. We discussed a few such titles including:

  • Fate/Stay Night
  • Fate/Hollow Ataraxia
  • Fate/Grand Order

Recommended Start Order

  1. Fate/Stay Night - Visual Novel
  2. Fate/Hollow Ataraxia - Visual Novel
  3. Fate Zero
  4. Any of the animes afterward
Fate Series

Fate is an Urban Fantasy action franchise, part of the greater Nasuverse created by Nasu Kinoko and Takeuchi Takashi. Originally just a series of self-contained material, the Fate franchise very quickly eclipsed the rest of Type-Moon's works and came into its own as a standalone series. - Excerpt taken from TV Tropes, linked above.
